WONTON NOODLE RECIPE

Back

DESCRIPTION

The dish is popular in Southern China, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore and Thailand. The dish usually consists of egg noodles served in a hot broth, garnished with leafy vegetables and wonton dumplings.

Ingredietns

  • Wonton Noodles
  • Wonton
  • Bok choy
  • Green onion
  • shrimp

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables & green onions
  2. Cook noddles, let it boil in water for about 60 seconds
  3. Add water and chicken broth and bring it to a boil
  4. Cook shrimp for a minute, transfer bok choy
  5. Cook wontons for 3-5 minutes then plate
