FRIED RICE RECIPE

Back

DESCRIPTION

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.

Ingredietns

  • Cooked Rice
  • Beaten eggs
  • Spring onions
  • Scallions
  • Oil & soy sauce

Steps

  1. Beat eggs into a flat omelet, 30s per side
  2. Cook protein with green onions, garlic and ginger
  3. Add tons of fresh vegetables, cook for 2 to 4 minutes
  4. Stir fry rice in oil. Toss rice around for even coating with oil
  5. Return all ingredients to the wok and stir frequently
